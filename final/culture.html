Bathukamma

Bathukamma is a vibrant floral festival celebrated by women across Telangana. They arrange colorful flowers in a cone shape and gather in groups to sing and dance, symbolizing female energy, nature, and community bonding.

Vinayaka Chaturthi

Vinayaka Chaturthi celebrates the birth of Lord Ganesha. In Telangana, especially Hyderabad, large Ganesha idols are installed in homes and public places. Devotees offer prayers, sweets, and perform cultural activities. The festival ends with the idol immersion (Nimajjanam) in lakes, symbolizing Ganesha's return. It's a celebration of devotion, unity, and culture.
