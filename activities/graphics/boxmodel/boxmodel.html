What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags create elements that start on a new line and take up full width, while inline tags stay within text flow and use only necessary space.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin is the space outside an element.

border is its outline.

padding is the space between the content and the border.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

CSS uses absolute and relative units for measurement. Absolute units like px, cm, mm, in, pt, and pc have fixed sizes. Relative units adjust based on context, including % for parent-based sizing, em and rem for font-based scaling, and vw, vh, vmin, and vmax for viewport-based sizing.

When would you use a figure tag?

The figure tag is used to group images or media with captions.
