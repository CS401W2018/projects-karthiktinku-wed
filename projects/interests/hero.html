About virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is one of the greatest cricketers of all time. Born in 1988 in India, he has dominated international cricket with his exceptional batting skills, leadership, and unmatched consistency. Known for his aggressive playing style and incredible fitness, Kohli has set multiple records in all formats of the game. He has captained the Indian national team, leading them to many victories, and continues to inspire young cricketers worldwide.

Notable Achievements

Fastest to 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 ODI Runs
Virat Kohli became the fastest batsman to achieve these milestones, showcasing his dominance in One Day Internationals.
2018 ICC Cricketer of the Year
Named the ICC Cricketer of the Year for his outstanding performances across all formats.
Indian Premier League (IPL) Success
As a key player for Royal Challengers Bangalore, Kohli has been one of the highest run-scorers in IPL history.
